Guilt Triage Journal · Say No Without Guilt
The Five Questions
Hear it. Understand it. Decide — don't obey.
Date
Time
Before you begin

Take one slow breath. You don't have to fix this feeling — only understand it. Answer honestly, not perfectly.

1
The Guilt
What am I feeling guilty about? Describe the situation specifically.
2
The Source
Where does this guilt come from? Check what fits.
3
The Truth
Not what guilt says — what is the most accurate, fair assessment? Have I actually done something wrong?
4
The Feeling
Can I feel this guilt and still be okay? Can I hold it without acting on it?
5
The Decision
Knowing the source, knowing the truth, feeling the feeling — what do I actually want to do?
Guilt is information, not instruction.
You heard it. Now you decide.
